Eirling Haaland

One of the people to look out for during the summer transfer window is Eirling Haaland.
After another brilliant season so far after scoring 21 goals in 21 games
it doesn't come a surprise to most that Haaland is at the top of most clubs summer targets.
The two Manchester giants, Manchester United and Manchester City are amongst many clubs
trying to get his signature. Will we see Haaland leave this summer or does his heart belong
in Dortmund.

Christiano Ronaldo

Is Ronaldo on the move?
The Eye Of Football understands that Juventus will make Ronaldo available for £25 million
this summer, as according to reports Juventus regret purchasing the player in 2018 because he is
too expensive. It is believed that Juventus pay Ronaldo £1 million after every goal scored.
You can understand why Juventus would regret that decision after he has scored 97 goals since arriving
at the Allianz Arena. I won't be surprised to see a number of clubs from Europes top 5 leagues lining up
to get his signature.
